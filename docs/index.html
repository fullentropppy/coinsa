Privacy Policy

App: Coinsa
Effective date: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coinsa ("the App") handles user data.

1. Data Collection

The App is designed to minimize data collection.

Coinsa does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

All data entered by the user (such as trips, locations, and expenses) is stored locally on the device and optionally in the user’s private iCloud storage.

2. iCloud (CloudKit)

The App uses Apple CloudKit to store and sync user data.

All data is stored in the user’s private iCloud database and is associated only with the user’s Apple ID.

The developer of the App does not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses a third-party service to retrieve currency exchange rates.

Requests sent to this service do not include any personal data and are limited to technical information such as currency pairs.

4. Data Usage

User data is used solely within the App for:

- Tracking trip expenses
- Calculating budgets and analytics
- Displaying currency conversions

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

The App does not use advertising or tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

Data stored in iCloud is protected by Apple’s security infrastructure. Local data is stored securely on the user’s device.

7. User Control

Users have full control over their data. Data can be deleted at any time within the App.

Removing the App will remove local data. iCloud data can be managed through Apple ID settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
