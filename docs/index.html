Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect personal identifiable information. All data is stored locally or in the user’s private iCloud.

2. iCloud (CloudKit)

The App uses CloudKit to store data in the user’s private iCloud database. The developer does not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Hexarate API to retrieve currency exchange rates. No personal data is sent.

4. Data Usage

Data is used only for tracking expenses and calculating budgets.

5. Data Sharing

No data is sold or shared.

6. Contact

For questions, contact the developer.
